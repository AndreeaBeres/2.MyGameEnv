In need of a hand of missing achievements of how to obtain it? Here's how.

Little Corn Cadet

Story related. Beat the Tutorial Level.

XP-ansion Pak

Story related as you're exploring around the world.

Smoking Kills

Attack the Soda Machine and stay on top of it. You have to let it faint to obtain it.

Private Screw'l

Refer to the maps inside the Guide section to obtain one.

Corn Syrup

2 ways to obtain it. One inside Some Other Place, or by collecting 5 Caps around Hollow's Hollow.

Hollow's Hollow

Some Other Place

Heroes in a Whole Shell

Many people didn't know how to obtain this, but this is a simple one to do. Lure the turtle near the Broken Staircase back to near Knirt's Shop to reunite with it's friend.

Highdive

Tip: before facing the boss in Inside the Tree, go to a high point and fall in the water bellow.

...I'm a Lasagna Hog

Story related. Defeat Owlloh and restore Mayor Hollow.

Magical Tetnis Challenge

Must have the Drill ability to do it. Go to The Park and dig under one of the socks then attack it from underneath.

MaXPower

By this, you must collect all the XP everywhere. Refer to the Guide for this.

Get N(achos) or Get Out

Story related. Climb The Tower and watch the cutscene.

Feast Fit For a Kid

This could take some practice to obtain 20 cans. You only have a limited amount of mistakes to do (which is 3 missing cans).

If you already beat the Mirror (Catch the Cans), you can replay inside Anxiety Tower at XP Lvl 5.

Anxiety Attack

Only one life all the way to the top, and barely enough health to make it. I recommend to have acquired all Health Upgrades to ease up while doing this.

As you progress through, you can find the last 2 Void Screws in which are hard to miss.

Annoyed the Void

Use a Void Screw.

High Bread Heaven

Use all 11 Void Screws.
