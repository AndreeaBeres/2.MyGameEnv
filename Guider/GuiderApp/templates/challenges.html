You've probably completed the game a few times. Why not consider to do a challenge?

Vsync

This option can be activated before you start the game. It's making the game speed double, requiring you to position the character a lot. It's challenging.

Keyboarder

Hear me out about that. Yes, you can play with Keyboard, but some notes say don't do it, as it's painful or if you cannot reach your controller. It takes a bit of time to get used to it. The only problem is you cannot delete the savefile if you want to start all over.

Hardcore

Now, this challenge requires you dedication and pure skill to achieve 0 deaths the entire gameplay. Although there are parts in which can be difficult even to try to keep yourself away from dying, having enough Health at all times is essential to this one. It's all about matter of trial and error.

Speedrunner

How fast can you go? For this one you need to practice your tricks for hours to attempt to do it right.
